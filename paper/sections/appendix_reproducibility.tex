\section{Reproducibility Notes}

The recommended clean-room commands are maintained in
\texttt{REPRODUCIBILITY.md}. A CPU-only smoke path is available through
\texttt{make smoke}. GPU mini reproduction is available through
\texttt{make reproduce-mini} and requires cached or downloadable Hugging Face
weights plus \texttt{--allow-real-models}. Full reproduction of all reference
cells requires more GPU time and storage.
