\section{Results and Findings}

Table~\ref{tab:headline-results} summarizes the current headline metrics from
the repository's CSV artifacts. The target sub-1B models struggle with
NOT\_FOUND abstention under generation: base SmolVLM2 500M has a 0.98
false-answer rate on controlled NOT\_FOUND and 1.0 on manual real-document
NOT\_FOUND. The 100-step LoRA PoC improves controlled false-answer rate to
0.10 and manual real-document false-answer rate to 0.6235, while answerable-only
DocMiniBench exact match stays approximately flat.

The same-family SmolVLM2 2.2B reference improves transfer abstention compared
with base SmolVLM2 500M, reaching 0.4353 manual real-document false-answer and
0.0 OOD sanity false-answer. Qwen2.5-VL 3B remains the strongest external
ceiling, especially on manual real-document NOT\_FOUND.
