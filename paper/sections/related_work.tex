\section{Related Work / Context}

Document VQA benchmarks such as DocVQA, ChartQA, OCR-style extraction tasks, and
infographic QA motivate the capability slices used here: exact OCR spans,
layout-bound key-value extraction, table lookup, numeric chart reasoning, and
domain-specific terms. The repository does not attempt to reproduce a full
leaderboard. Instead, it packages a small benchmark and evaluation harness that
can be rerun on constrained hardware.

The NOT\_FOUND setting is closest to answerability and hallucination-control
evaluation. In this project, the original DocMiniBench-v0 NOT\_FOUND slice was
audited and demoted because it used fixed templates on a weak source slice and
did not mechanically prove absence. Controlled synthetic NOT\_FOUND and manual
real-document NOT\_FOUND are therefore reported separately.
