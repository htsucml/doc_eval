\section{Future Work}

The most important next step is collecting verified real-document negative
examples with explicit field inventories or OCR/annotation evidence gates.
Future modeling work should evaluate answerability verifiers, OCR evidence
gates, distillation from the Qwen2.5-VL 3B ceiling, and preference-style
abstention tuning. Any likelihood gate or reranker should be calibrated on held
out data and reported with answerable over-abstention.

Longer SFT sweeps should mix controlled positives, controlled negatives, and
verified real-document negatives while preserving strict separation from all
evaluation images and rows.
