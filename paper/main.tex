\begin{abstract}
We present a bounded, reproducible document vision-language model evaluation
focused on compact models and abstention behavior. The benchmark combines
DocMiniBench-v0 traditional document QA capabilities with controlled synthetic,
manual real-document, and non-document out-of-distribution NOT\_FOUND tests.
Experiments compare sub-1B SmolVLM-family targets, a bounded LoRA/SFT
methodology proof of concept, and clearly separated $>$1B reference-only
models. Results are generated from committed scripts and existing CSV/JSONL
artifacts; no result in this report is manually invented.
\end{abstract}
